Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to Gymeal. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal information. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your data when you use our nutrition and fitness tracking application.

Your privacy matters. We collect only the data necessary to provide you with a great experience, and we never sell your personal information to third parties.

Data We Collect

Account Information

  • Email address (for authentication)
  • Display name (optional)
  • Profile photo (optional)

Health & Fitness Data

  • Meals and nutritional information you log
  • Workout sessions and exercise data
  • Body measurements (weight, body fat %, etc.)
  • Nutritional goals and preferences

Apple HealthKit Data

With your explicit permission, we may read and write:

  • Workout data (duration, calories burned, exercise type)
  • Body measurements (weight, body fat percentage)

HealthKit data is stored locally on your device and synced to our servers only with your consent.

Voice & Photo Analysis

Our premium features use AI to help you log meals:

  • Voice Input: Audio recordings are processed by OpenAI to extract meal information, then immediately deleted
  • Photo Analysis: Food photos are analyzed by OpenAI's Vision API to estimate calories, then immediately deleted

How We Use Your Data

  • Provide and improve our nutrition and fitness tracking services
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Generate personalized insights and recommendations
  • Send important service notifications

Third-Party Services

We use the following trusted services:

Firebase (Google) — Secure cloud storage and authentication

OpenAI — AI-powered meal analysis (voice and photo features)

Apple HealthKit — Health data synchronization (with your permission)

Data Security

We implement industry-standard security measures:

  • All data transmission is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Data at rest is encrypted in Firebase
  • Authentication is handled securely by Firebase Auth
  • We regularly review and update our security practices

Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data at any time within the app
  • Correct any inaccurate information
  • Delete your account and all associated data
  • Export your data (feature coming soon)
  • Withdraw consent for optional data processing

Data Retention

We retain your data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account:

  • All personal data is permanently deleted within 30 days
  • Anonymized, aggregated data may be retained for analytics

Children's Privacy

Gymeal is not intended for children under 13 years of age. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If you are a parent or guardian and believe your child has provided us with personal information, please contact us.

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any material changes by posting the new policy in the app and updating the "Last updated" date.
