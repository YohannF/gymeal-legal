Privacy Policy

Last updated: December 30, 2025

Gymeal is a nutrition and workout tracking application developed by Yohann Félicité ("we", "our", "us"). This privacy policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your personal data.

1. Data We Collect

1.1 Account Data

  • Email address (for account creation and authentication)
  • Display name (optional)

1.2 Nutrition Data

  • Logged meals (foods, quantities, dates)
  • Custom recipes
  • Custom foods created by you
  • Nutritional goals

1.3 Workout Data

  • Workout sessions (exercises, sets, reps, weights)
  • Custom workout templates
  • Custom exercises

1.4 Health Data (Apple HealthKit)

If you enable HealthKit sync, we may read and write:

  • Body weight
  • Body fat percentage
  • Workout data
  • Nutritional data (calories, protein, carbs, fat)
Important: This data stays on your device in Apple's Health app and is not sent to our servers.

1.5 Voice and Photo Data (Premium Features)

  • Voice input: Your voice recordings are temporarily processed to transcribe your meals. Transcriptions are sent to OpenAI for analysis.
  • Photo analysis: Photos of your meals are sent to OpenAI to estimate calories. Photos are not stored on our servers.

2. How We Use Your Data

We use your data only to:

  • Provide app functionality
  • Sync your data across your devices
  • Improve user experience
  • Send important communications (service changes, security)

We never sell your personal data.

3. Third-Party Services

3.1 Firebase (Google)

We use Firebase for:

  • Authentication: Secure account management
  • Firestore: Cloud data storage

Your data is stored on Google's servers, protected by their security infrastructure.
Firebase Privacy Policy

3.2 OpenAI

For voice and photo analysis features:

  • Voice transcriptions and photos are sent to OpenAI APIs
  • OpenAI processes this data according to their privacy policy
  • Data is not used to train their models (API usage)

OpenAI Privacy Policy

3.3 Apple HealthKit

  • HealthKit sync is optional and requires your explicit permission
  • HealthKit data stays on your device
  • We cannot access your HealthKit data without your permission

4. Data Security

We protect your data through:

  • Encryption of data in transit (HTTPS/TLS)
  • Secure authentication via Firebase
  • Storage of sensitive keys in iOS Keychain
  • Data access limited to your account only

5. Data Retention

  • Your data is retained as long as your account is active
  • You can delete your account at any time from the app
  • Account deletion results in deletion of all your data

6. Your Rights

You have the right to:

  • Access your personal data
  • Correct inaccurate data
  • Delete your account and all your data
  • Export your data (feature coming soon)
  • Withdraw consent for optional features (HealthKit, voice, photo)

To exercise these rights, contact us at: feliciteyohann@gmail.com

7. Children

Gymeal is not intended for children under 13. We do not knowingly collect data from children.

8. Changes

We may update this policy. Significant changes will be notified in the app.
